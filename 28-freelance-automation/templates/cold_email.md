# Cold Email Template

## Subject Line
[Short, specific, benefit-oriented - avoid spam triggers]

---

Hi [Name],

[Opening line - personal observation about their company/product/challenge. Show you did research.]

[The Problem] I noticed [specific pain point or opportunity they're missing].

[Your Solution] I recently helped [similar company/role] achieve [specific result with metric] by [brief approach].

[Social Proof] [One line credibility - clients, results, or recognition]

[CTA] Would it make sense to spend 15 minutes exploring if I could help [Company] with [specific outcome]?

Either way, [compliment their work / wish them well].

Best,
[Your Name]
[Title] | [Website]

---

**P.S.** [Optional - add urgency, bonus value, or relevant link]

---

**Email Guidelines:**
- Subject line: 3-7 words, lowercase looks more personal
- Body: Under 150 words
- One clear CTA (don't give multiple options)
- Follow up 3-4 days later if no response
- Max 3 follow-ups total
