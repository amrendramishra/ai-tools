# LinkedIn Outreach Template

## Subject Line
[Short, specific, curiosity-inducing - max 60 chars]

## Message

Hi [Name],

[1 sentence personal connection - mutual connection, their recent post, company news]

[1-2 sentences about what you noticed they might need / their challenge]

[1-2 sentences on how you've solved this exact problem before, with a brief metric]

[Soft CTA - not selling, just offering value]

Would love to share [a quick insight / relevant case study / my approach] if you're open to a brief chat.

Best,
[Your Name]
[Your Title]

---

**Tips:**
- Keep under 300 characters for connection requests
- For InMail, can be longer (up to 1900 chars)
- Always personalize the first line
- Don't pitch in the first message if possible
